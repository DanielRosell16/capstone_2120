What are the first steps in planning your vacation?

  • Pick the destination
  • Make a budget
  • Decide on if you are driving or flying
  • Figure out if you want to stay at a hotel or AirBnB

Let's look a bit deeper at this list

Picking the place can be hard. Do you like the beach and ocean? Do you like woods and forests? Do you like sand dunes or mountains? Once you figure that out, it will be a little bit easier. Another HUGE question would be do you want to stay in the country you're in now or go out of the country?

Budgeting is easy to plan, but hard to stick to. The budget needs to encompass the hotels/Air BnB place, the food, the activities, and souvenirs. That is the stuff that needs to be in the budget but then there are other things that should be in the budget that are unexpected expenses like extra clothes, hospital trips, medicines and other things.

Deciding on whether to drive or fly to your dream vacation is an important decision. Driving could be fun with freinds or family because it allows quality time during the road trip however the drive time will have to be taken out the vacation time at your dream vacation location. Flying will allow you to get to your vacation destination sooner. That can be fun too! Getting to your destination sooner will help get you relaxed sooner and sometimes can be cheaper then driving.

A hotel or AirBnB is a huge decision for many reasons. The hotels can be fun places to stay at espically if there is a pool. However the only meal they offer is breakfast and it is usually small amounts of food but all you can eat. Hotels can also get expensive if you have are on a long vacation. An AirBnB offers different options. They provide more privacy then hotels do and they usually come with a kitchen so you can buy groceries and cook your meals everyday which would save money because you wouldn't have to spend money on eating out for every meal other then breakfast. The AirBnB option also can be cheaper as they sometimes offer a disconted price for the longer someone stays at the AirBnB.
